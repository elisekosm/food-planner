### **1. Lemon Garlic Shrimp with Asparagus & Quinoa**
**Description:** A bright, zesty one-pan dish with juicy shrimp, tender asparagus, and fluffy quinoa. Packed with protein and fiber.
**Ingredients:**
- Â½ cup quinoa, rinsed
- 1 cup water
- 12 oz shrimp, peeled & deveined
- 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 2-inch pieces
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 lemon (zest + juice)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Â½ tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
- Â½ tsp salt
- Â¼ tsp black pepper
**Instructions:**
1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions (simmer for 15 mins, then fluff). Set aside.
2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add shrimp, garlic, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Cook 2-3 mins until shrimp turn pink.
3. Add asparagus and lemon juice. Cook another 4-5 mins until asparagus is tender.
4. Stir in cooked quinoa, lemon zest, and adjust seasoning. Serve hot.

### **2. Chickpea & Spinach Coconut Curry (One-Pan)**
**Description:** A creamy, aromatic curry with chickpeas, spinach, and coconut milk for a satisfying plant-based meal.
**Ingredients:**
- 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained & rinsed
- 2 cups fresh spinach
- 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk (light or full-fat)
- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper to taste
- Fresh cilantro (optional, for garnish)
**Instructions:**
1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. SautÃ© onion and garlic until soft (3-4 mins).
2. Add curry powder, turmeric, and cumin. Stir for 30 secs until fragrant.
3. Pour in coconut milk, then add chickpeas. Simmer for 10 mins.
4. Stir in spinach until wilted (2-3 mins). Season with salt & pepper. Garnish with cilantro.
